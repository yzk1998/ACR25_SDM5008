\section{Scene Design Description}

The custom scene consists of five cuboid-shaped platforms. 
Each object has specific dimensions, positions, and color tones as described below:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{CuboidDeformable1} \\
    Size: 4.5 m × 4.5 m × 0.07 m (length × width × height) \\
    Position: x = 0.0 m, y = –5.0 m, z = 0.07 m \\
    Physics: Kinematic enabled, mass = 1.0 kg \\
    Color: Light green (R = 0.1, G = 1.0, B = 0.1) \\
    Purpose: Large flat platform.

    \item \textbf{CuboidDeformable2} \\
    Size: 4.0 m × 4.0 m × 0.07 m \\
    Position: x = 0.0 m, y = –6.0 m, z = 0.15 m \\
    Physics: Kinematic enabled, mass = 1.0 kg \\
    Color: Medium green (R = 0.3, G = 1.0, B = 0.3) \\
    Purpose: Slightly smaller platform placed after CuboidDeformable1.

    \item \textbf{CuboidDeformable3} \\
    Size: 3.0 m × 3.0 m × 0.07 m \\
    Position: x = 0.0 m, y = –7.0 m, z = 0.20 m \\
    Physics: Kinematic enabled, mass = 1.0 kg \\
    Color: Light green (R = 0.5, G = 1.0, B = 0.5) \\
    Purpose: Intermediate platform for stepping or obstacle crossing.

    \item \textbf{CuboidDeformable4} \\
    Size: 2.0 m × 2.0 m × 0.07 m \\
    Position: x = 0.0 m, y = –8.0 m, z = 0.25 m \\
    Physics: Kinematic enabled, mass = 1.0 kg \\
    Color: Pale green (R = 0.7, G = 1.0, B = 0.7) \\
    Purpose: Smaller elevated step.

    \item \textbf{CuboidDeformable5} \\
    Size: 1.0 m × 1.0 m × 0.07 m \\
    Position: x = 0.0 m, y = –9.0 m, z = 0.30 m \\
    Physics: Kinematic enabled, mass = 1.0 kg \\
    Color: Very light green (R = 0.9, G = 1.0, B = 0.9) \\
    Purpose: Final small stepping platform.
\end{itemize}
